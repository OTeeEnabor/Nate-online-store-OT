SA Fashion Week 2019

26 July 2019

The 2019 Free State Fashion Week will be held at the Sun Windmill in Bloemfontein from 19 to 21 September 2019. This year's theme is 'Our Roots Conquer'....

This year, the designers are challenged to go beyond what is usual, regular or customary as they are marked to conquer through their African heritage. Designers will be tasked to focus on this theme ‘Our Roots Conquer’ and combine it with African culture and heritage.


For more details and developments about the event visit www.freestatefashionweek.co.za
